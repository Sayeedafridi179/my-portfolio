About Me

I'm a creative graphic designer based in Dubai, UAE, with over a decade of hands-on experience turning ideas into compelling visual language. From concept to production, I specialise in print design, brand identity, packaging, and digital graphics — built on a foundation of precision and creative thinking.



Currently working as Graphic Designer & Production Manager at Golden Future Advertising, Dubai. I've worn many hats — from running my own print shop in Bangladesh to leading design at advertising agencies. My work spans flyers, posters, sticker designs, die cuts, and large-format output.



I thrive in fast-paced, client-facing environments where consistency, creativity, and deadlines all matter equally. Fluent in English, Bangla, and Hindi — I communicate design as clearly as I create it.
